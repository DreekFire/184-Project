Final Project Report: Strandbeest Simulation

Marcus Cheung, Derek Guo, Pranav Kolluri, Rohan



Abstract

We will create a simulation of a strandbeest walking along a beach. We will primarily focus on the simulation of the physical joints of the strandbeest and the visual rendering of the sand. If we have extra time, we may also incorporate physically realistic procedural generation of the sand and have it move under then strandbeest.
